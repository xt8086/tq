Quick Start
  1. Install: curl -fsSL https://wondermotor.com/install.sh | bash
  2. List models: tq list
  3. Search & download: tq search "qwen2.5 coder 7b" then tq download <model>
  4. Serve: tq serve 1 — TurboQuant auto-configured.
